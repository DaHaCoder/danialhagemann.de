This is the time dependent Schrödinger equation.

$$ \mathrm{i} \hbar \frac{\partial}{\partial t} \Psi(\boldsymbol{r},t) = \biggl(- \frac{\hbar^2}{2m} \boldsymbol{\nabla}^2 + \hat{V}(\boldsymbol{r}) \biggr) \Psi(\boldsymbol{r},t).$$
