These are the Maxwell equations:

$$
\div{\vb*{E}} = 4 \pi \rho
$$

$$
\div{\vb*{B}} = 0
$$

$$
\curl{\vb*{E}} = - \frac{1}{c} \pdv{\vb*{B}}{t}
$$

$$
\curl{\vb*{B}} = \frac{1}{c} \biggl(4 \pi \vb*{j} + \pdv{\vb*{E}}{t} \biggr)
$$
